Handcrafted Haven


"Connecting Craftsmanship with Conscience"


Handcrafted Haven is a digital marketplace startup designed for the modern artisan. It provides a warm, organic space for creators to showcase their craftsmanship, share their personal stories, and connect with buyers who value quality over mass production.
✨ Project Overview

This project was developed as a full-stack web application solution for a local artisan marketplace. The platform bridges the gap between independent creators and a global audience, focusing on visual storytelling and high-end user experience.

🎯 Target Audience
Artisans: Skilled creators looking for a premium storefront that emphasizes the "handmade" nature of their goods.
Conscious Consumers: Buyers looking for unique, high-quality items with a clear origin and story.

 Design Philosophy
The design of Handcrafted Haven is built to feel "organic" and "premium."
Typography: We use Playfair Display (Serif) for headings to evoke a sense of tradition and elegance, paired with Inter (Sans-serif) for clean, modern readability.
Color Palette:
blue : Represents nature and sustainability.
black : Represents earth, clay, and the raw materials of crafting.
white : A soft, warm background to reduce eye strain.
